WISDOM TODAY

We emphasize wisdom because we believe this is about more than finding a quick-fix in technology or politics – it is about finding something deeper within ourselves and our societies.

WHAT WE DO

  • ENVISIONING
    We are committed to open-mindedly engage in a conversation about a new big vision. The Art / Earth / Tech Institute exists to identify the principles that can guide us to this wiser vision.

  • COMMUNITY
    We believe a wiser way of living requires us to go beyond individualism and collectivism. Our Hubs experiment with a new path of living together as community by connecting cities with rural places.

  • livelihood
    The ability to have a community depends on livelihood on which we spend much of our lives. Art / Earth / Tech thrives to create well business culture so we can materially and emotionally support ourselves.
